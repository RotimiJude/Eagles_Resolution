Lead Researcher, Eagles Research Solutions

Dr. Adedokun Oluwafemi Ojelabi

Dr. Adedokun O. Ojelabi is the Lead Researcher at Eagles Research Solutions (ERS), driving the organisation's research excellence with deep expertise in research design, statistical modelling, evidence synthesis, and planning, monitoring & evaluation. He is adept at turning complex data into clear insights that advance policy, practice, and academic knowledge. His work spans a range of high-impact research, including studies on health-related quality of life, systematic reviews in chronic disease measurement, and quantitative methods for real-world data analysis. Notable publications include work on predictors of quality of life in sickle cell disease and the application of health-related quality of life models in chronic conditions. Dr. Ojelabi is proficient in SPSS, AMOS, R and STATA and brings rich experience from academia and applied research. With a Ph.D. in Public Health from the University of Sunderland, United Kingdom and a passion for rigorous, ethical inquiry, he leads ERS's commitment to transforming research into reliable evidence with real-world impact and ensuring process and outcomes align with global best practices.
